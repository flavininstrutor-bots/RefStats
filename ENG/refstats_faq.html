‚ñº

We collect public data and present it in a visual and intuitive way, including: referee card and foul averages, team performance, comparative charts, and more.

üîß Features

How does the search work?

‚ñº

On the "TODAY'S MATCHES" page, use the search bar to find matches by:

  • Team name: Type "Flamengo" or "Real Madrid"
  • Referee name: Type the referee's name
  • League: Type "Premier League" or "La Liga"

Use the navigation buttons to move between found results.

What are the referee profile filters?

‚ñº

Referees are classified into three profiles based on their history:

  • üî¥ Strict: High average of cards per game
  • üü° Moderate: Balanced average of cards
  • üü¢ Lenient: Low average of cards

You can filter matches by clicking the profile buttons in the search bar.

How do I access matches from previous dates?

‚ñº

Click on "HISTORY" in the menu. You'll see a calendar where you can select any date with available matches.

Days with available analyses appear marked in green with the "MATCH DAY" indication.

What does the comparison chart mean?

‚ñº

The chart shows a visual comparison between:

  • Referee average: Average number of yellow cards the referee gives per game
  • Team averages: Average number of cards each team receives per game

This helps quickly visualize if the match tends to have more or fewer cards.

üìä Data and Sources

Where does the data come from?

‚ñº

Data is collected mainly from SofaScore, WhoScored, FlashScore, and other public sports statistics platforms.

We process and organize this data to present it in a more visual and intuitive way.

Is the data reliable?

‚ñº

We strive to provide accurate data, but we do not guarantee absolute accuracy. Errors or outdated information may exist.

We always recommend verifying information from multiple sources before making decisions.

How often is the data updated?

‚ñº

Analyses are generated daily for the day's matches. The referee's history considers their most recent games available in the database.

Why don't some referees have data?

‚ñº

This can happen when:

  • The referee has not yet been confirmed for the match
  • It's a new referee with little history
  • Data is not available from the source

In these cases, we show only available team information.
